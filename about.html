Taiwan PoPo Travel Service Introduction


About Us

We are a both outbound and inbound company. Outbound is 90% of our business. We founded in 2024. however, we have more than 10 years of outbound experience.

For outgound, we focus on managing certain people, which are a group of local communities. most of them are senior people. These tourists are mostly join our tours each year.

These people go aboard few times a year. This year they may go Europe and Japan. The next year they may go China, Korea or Australia. They usually go aboard twice a year.

Sometimes we ask Taiwan travel agents to help us with the tours in foreign country services. (They have a good price.)

Our tourists like a little bit hiking, eating, and sight seeing. Most tours are tailer made.

We have a few FIT customers. They ask us to provide local hotels and trips; if we have some factsheets from your hotel, we will recommend it to our customers.

If you are local travel agent, please provide your contact information to us. Based on your location, we provide your direct contact to our customers. We are not a specialist to your area. We need your advice or our customers can conact you directly.

Inbound services are 10% of total business. we get these services by chance. Local people or travel agents provide the information to us. They are usually their relatives or friends.

If there is a small amount of money to pay to other countries, we use Paypal or credit card to pay.

Customer Type: We have a big group to go aboard every 2 months; a few 4~6 person groups go aboard each month.
